Introduction

It all started thousands of years ago when a lone wolf had to watch his entire clan die due to the pandemic that hit the land. The angry but determined wolve howled through the woods as he ran all night, day, week, and years-long, searching for greener pasture.

He didn't just fit into any other pack, for he was unique was seen as a threat. the lone wolf continued in his quest to never settle for less. That brought him to the entrance of a very magnificent gate. Inside this gate is an endless pool of fertile lands, game, and habitation; it was the dream of every pack: The Metaverse.

The excited wolf went back to his clan to tell the good news to the elders of the pack who remained unsmitten from the pandemic that broke in their land.

After several meetings held under the full moon, information got to the 111 Alpha wolves that enemies had somehow gained access to the portal that led to this land of every wolf's dream.

Determined and battle-ready, the 111 Alpha wolves began their journey through the thick, dark woods that will soon teleport them into the metaverse.

Will the enemies be ready for the Alpha wolves when they come growling?

Roadmap

  • Phase 1:
    Welcome to the pack.

    Our goal is to develop a team of like-minded, business-oriented wolves to ensure the success of packs and assets.

    Our team aspires to be made of Alpha wolves who share a common goal and preserve the integrity of the pack. The quality of our sigil is a vital component of our asset; hence, we have chosen the best species to represent our wolves.

    The qualities and technicalities of our project are still in progress, but you should look forward to the announcement of our release date.

  • Phase 2:
    Access invaluable knowledge from the pack

    Our pack members will have access to vital, exclusive information concerning CRYPTOCURRENCIES, NFTs, CRYPTO GAMING, INVESTING, REAL ESTATE, METAVERSE, and more, from our biggest investors.

    Top members of the pack include entrepreneurs, startup founders, NFT collectors, renowned investors, and whales who will share indispensable value to members through weekly Zoom calls, Investment and Strategy Advice, and Free Access to the Best Online Courses.

  • Phase 3:
    Increase the worth of your NFT

    Any lucky wolf who mints a rare NFT can double, triple, and even get up 10x of their investment in a few days. But the Alphas want something better for their pack to help increase the worth of their NFT using their skills.

    The Alpha wolves reward their members based on their best score in a series of three simple games. Each reward is in the form of a weapon; the market value of your NFT increases at once based on the rewards you earn from your games.

  • Phase 4:
    conquer the metaverse

    Together with its pack, the Alpha Wolves have come to establish their presence in the entire woods of the metaverse. Members of our pack will have access to all exclusive activities such as casinos, games, etc., and provide an avenue for the masterminds of Web 3.0 to share business ideas and opportunities and maintain their position as the strongest pack in the metaverse.

  • Phase 5:
    Community Support

    As a member of the Alpha Wolves pack, you get to have access to private investment opportunities from the biggest members of the pack. Some investment opportunities include getting access to early-round investments in startups, real estate market deals, IDOs, ICOs, angel investing, and many more.

    The pack believes in the growth of each member. Every member has the right to vote and choose profitable projects through our Decentralized Autonomous Organization. The individual and collective growth of the pack remains our priority.

  • Phase 6:
    Access to our Premium Merch

    Every member of our pack will have access to our exclusive premium merchandise, each member of the team will have an original piece with their unique NFT numbers on their merch.

  • Phase 7:
    Loyalty to the pack

    Again, the singular objective of our pack is to establish a community of wolves where loyalty is our watchword. Long-term holders will be rewarded with many utilities and unexpected gifts at the least expected time.

    We encourage our sellers to know that the “regret of missing out” is worse than the “fear of missing out”. This way, we build a community of fierce wolves ready to take on the metaverse.
